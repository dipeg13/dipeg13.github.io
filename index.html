Research Interests

My research interests include:

  • Machine Learning: Nowadays growth of data availability has led machine learning applications' efficiency and accuracy to an exponential growth. My main interests focuses around Clustering and Time Series Analysis and Forecasting
  • Automated Machine Leaning: Machine learning tasks demand data science specialization and domain knowledge, preventing some practitioners from taking part in the ML/AI era. AutoML aims to democratize machine learning, allowing everyone, independently of their field of expertize, to carry out machine learning tasks by automating the task of the machine learning life-cycle. My main interests focuses around AutoML for clustering and time series forecasting.
  • Optimization: Optimization is the core concept of AutoML, aiming mainly at the problem of hyperparameter tuning. My main interests focuses around optimization using probabilistic frameworks, such as Bayesian Optimization.
